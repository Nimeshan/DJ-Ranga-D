About DJ RangaD

DJ Ranga D

DJ RangaD is a seasoned music maestro with over 15 years of experience spinning tunes and orchestrating unforgettable beats. Hailing originally from Sri Lanka, his passion for music led him on a journey across borders, from the vibrant nightlife of Dubai to the bustling scene of Australia.
With a knack for reading the crowd and an eclectic taste in music, DJ RangaD brings a unique flavor to every event he graces.
With each performance, DJ RangaD not only delivers an exceptional musical experience but also creates lasting memories that resonate with his audience long after the final song fades away.
With his international experience and innate talent, DJ RangaD continues to elevate the art of DJing, spreading joy and creating unforgettable memories wherever his musical journey takes him.

Where I Play

Weddings

In weddings, DJ RangaD understands the importance of curating the perfect playlist to accompany every moment, from the romantic first dance to the lively celebrations that follow. His seamless transitions between genres ensure that guests of all ages are captivated on the dance floor, making the wedding a truly memorable occasion for everyone involved.

Clubs and Bar

When the sun sets and the city comes alive, DJ RangaD takes center stage in the vibrant nightlife scene of clubs and bars. His dynamic mixes blend infectious beats with eclectic tunes, captivating audiences and keeping them grooving until the early hours of the morning. With his ability to gauge the energy of the crowd, he crafts an immersive musical journey that leaves club-goers craving for more.

Corporate Events

In the corporate world, DJ RangaD brings a touch of sophistication to events with his seamless blend of professionalism and entertainment. Whether it's a gala dinner, product launch, or corporate retreat, he sets the tone with his curated playlists and impeccable timing, ensuring that every moment is infused with the right atmosphere to leave a lasting impression on attendees.

DJ Lessons

DJ RangaD is also a mentor, sharing her wealth of knowledge and experience through DJ lessons. His passion for nurturing talent and fostering the next generation of DJs is evident in his personalized approach and dedication to the craft.
